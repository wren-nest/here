Sunday April 21st, 2024

Target. Spent 20 minutes walking in circles. Didn’t want to ask where they keep gloves. I found them eventually. Walked past them three times before I did. Walked past a high school friend. I don’t know if they recognised me.

Friday June 7th, 2024

Dollar store. Terrible quality notebook. It was only four dollars. Easier to write in something that doesn’t feel fancy. Nothing actually costs a dollar anymore. On Monday I’ll say this to him, and he’ll tell me that he was at the mall today too.

Saturday September 8th, 2024

I knew I would run into him. Or, I was hoping that I would. We had lunch together. Burritos. They kind of sucked. Tasted a little better with him sitting next to me.

Wednesday December 17th, 2024

We spent all afternoon just wandering around. I don’t remember what we were looking for, I don’t think we found it. I kept looking at him instead of the shops. I want to see more of his smile.
